About Me

Mavie Sommerfeld

I create individual tattoos with care and attention to detail. Blackwork, geometry and clean forms fascinate me above all – especially when they adapt naturally to the body. It's important to me that you feel comfortable and that we create a design together that feels right for you in the long term.
